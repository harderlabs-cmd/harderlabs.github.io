About HarderLabs

At HarderLabs, we believe that the smartest solutions come from harder thinking. Based in Adelaide, South Australia, HarderLabs is a multidisciplinary tech and design studio built to solve problems that others overlook — with clarity, creativity, and a hands-on approach.

Founded by Karl Harder, a skilled IT support engineer, CAD specialist, and design technician with over 20 years of real-world experience, HarderLabs blends technical knowledge with practical skills to support individuals, startups, trades, and small businesses with reliable, affordable solutions.

What makes HarderLabs different is our ability to bridge multiple disciplines under one roof. We're not just IT people. We're not just drafters. We're problem-solvers who understand how technology, design, and people work together.

With a foundation built on real experience, a personal touch, and a drive to make technology and design work for real people, we're not just another service provider — we're your creative, technical ally.
